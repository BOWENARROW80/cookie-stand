About Us

Welcome to Pat's Salmon Cookies happy and every expanding family business. Here we not only make cookies in the shape of salmon, but also are made with salmon. The fish are ground so fine that you can't even taste it. It's healthy and the ideal match for a steaming cup of coffee on a gray Seattle morning, afternoon, or whenever. It's the best of both worlds and what's more Pacific Northwest than that?
